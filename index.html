📋 Terms of Service

1. Acceptance of Terms

By registering on Trusted Hands, you agree to these terms in full. If you do not agree, do not use this platform. These terms apply to all users — customers and skilled workers alike.

2. Platform Role

Trusted Hands is a marketplace platform that connects customers with independent artisans. We do not employ artisans. We are not responsible for the quality of work delivered by any artisan. We facilitate connection, not the work itself.

3. Skilled Worker Obligations

Skilled workers must: carry valid ID when requested, communicate pricing clearly before starting work, arrive on time, maintain professional conduct, and complete jobs they accept. Violations may result in account suspension or permanent ban.

4. Customer Obligations

Customers must: treat workers with respect, pay agreed amounts promptly, not share worker contact details outside the platform without consent, and provide accurate job descriptions.

5. Subscriptions & Payments

Worker subscriptions are non-refundable once activated. Subscription pauses are limited to 3 days per billing period. Fraudulent payment receipts will result in immediate permanent ban and possible legal action.

6. Disputes

Disputes between customers and workers must be reported through the in-app support system within 48 hours of the job. Trusted Hands reserves the right to freeze accounts, withhold visibility, or mediate disputes at our sole discretion.

7. Prohibited Conduct

The following are strictly prohibited: harassment, fraud, misrepresentation of qualifications, collecting payment outside the platform after being connected through it, and any form of abuse. Violations result in immediate permanent ban.

8. Account Termination

We reserve the right to suspend or permanently ban any account at our discretion, with or without notice, for violations of these terms or behavior that harms the platform community.

9. Limitation of Liability

Trusted Hands shall not be liable for any damages arising from use of this platform, including but not limited to: job quality disputes, loss of income, property damage, or personal injury during any job arranged through this platform.

10. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Any disputes shall be resolved in courts of competent jurisdiction in Edo State, Nigeria.

🔒 Privacy Policy

What We Collect

We collect: full name, email address, phone number, location zone, profile photo, portfolio photos, payment receipt images, and chat messages. For skilled workers, we also collect trade category, bio, and booking history.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to: display your profile on the marketplace, facilitate communication between users, process subscription payments, improve platform features, and send you important platform notifications. We do not sell your data to any third party.

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely on Google Firebase. Profile text data is stored in Firestore with authentication required for all reads and writes. All images — including portfolio photos, profile photos, ID documents, receipts, and chat images — are stored in Firebase Storage with secure access tokens. No raw passwords are stored — authentication is handled entirely by Firebase Auth.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting support. Profile data can be updated at any time through the Edit Profile screen. Chat history is retained for platform safety.

Cookies & Sessions

We use browser session storage for login state only. Sessions expire when you close the browser. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies of any kind.

Contact

For privacy concerns or data deletion requests, contact us through the in-app support system or email: olaowyegideon@gmail.com

Who We Are

Trusted Hands is a Nigerian skilled worker marketplace founded in Ekpoma, Edo State. We connect customers with skilled, verified local workers across Edo, Delta and Ogun State — making it safe, easy and reliable to find the right person for any job.

Our Mission

To eliminate the risk of hiring unknown workers by building a platform where every artisan is verified, reviewed, and accountable — and every customer is protected.

How We Verify Skilled Workers

📸
Portfolio Review
Every worker must upload at least 2 real photos of past work before their profile goes live.
🪪
ID Verification
Workers submit a government-issued ID which is reviewed by our admin team before approval.
⭐
Real Reviews Only
Only customers with verified bookings can leave reviews — no fake ratings.
🛡️
Admin Oversight
Our team reviews every worker application and can suspend accounts that receive valid complaints.
